Roast Chicken Recipes

Description

Special pan friend then oven baked chicken, seaasoned with black pepper, salt, onion, lemon, thyme, rosemary and olive oil

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • OLive Oil
  • Black Pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Half an Onion
  • Half a Lemon
  • Sprinkle Thyme
  • Sprinkle of Rosemary

Steps

  1. Season the outside of the chicken with black pepper, salt and olive oil
  2. Rub Rosemary and Thyme to the inside of the chicken
  3. Place the chicken with the thigh side down and fry the dark meat for 10 to 15 minutes at low to medium heat.
  4. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees celsuis while the chicken is pan fried
  5. Stuff the onion and lemon into the chicken and place foil over the pan while opening some holes in the covering.
  6. Leave the chicken to cook for three hour, checking every 30 minutes. When the chicken skin is crispy and golden and the gravy has formed around it, you can take it out of the oven.
  7. Get a carving knife and ENjoy! :)
