Saag Recipes

Description

A lovely vegetarian dish beloved all across Asia. The crunchy fried goodiness of the Paneer goes beauitfully with the buttery saltiness freshness of the Saag

Serves 3 people with Naan

Time to make: 45 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of Paneer in small cubes (1"x1")
  • 500 grams Frozen Saag or fresh Saag according to taste
  • 2 to 3 Heaped Tablespoons of Ghee
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Pan fry the Paneer in Oil and Ghee until Golden Brown on a medium heat
  2. While the Paneer is being sauted, place the Saag into an Bowl and let it cook in its own juices
  3. After the Paneer is cooked, Remove the Panner into a seperate bowl of bowling water and salt. Using the leftover oil, fry on low heat the cumin seeds.
  4. As the cumin seeds start to change colour, Increase the heat to mid. Add garlic to fry.
  5. When the garlic turns golden brown, add the salt, trumeric and chilli powder and mix until the spices change colour slightly.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes and reduce until your left with a sort of tomatoey paste. You have now made Tarka.
  7. Add the Tarka to the the Saag and mix well. Then follow up with the Paneer and enjoy :)
