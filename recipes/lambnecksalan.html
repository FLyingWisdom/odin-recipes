Lamb Neck Salan Recipes

Description

A hearty meal loved by many in the northern parts of Pakistan and Afghanistan. THere's many ways to make it but here's my take. A slow cooked version that gives you delectably soft meat.

Serves 5 People with Naan or Roti

Time to make: 1 hour to cook. 4 hours to leave to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram of Lamb neck, cut into curry pieaces
  • 1 whole onion peeled
  • 4 to 5 bulbs of Garlic
  • Garam Masala
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Tomatoes paste
  • Ghee
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Garlic, chopped
  • Whole green chillis
  • Chopped Cilantro/Coriander

Steps

  1. Sear the Lack neck pieces in Oil, until each side is a bit more than golden brown.
  2. Place the seared neck into a cooking pot and submerged in boiling water and let cook on slow heat.
  3. Add Salt to taste, garam masala, black pepper, whole onion to the pot and let the meat cook under low heat
  4. After letting the meat cook enough that it can peel off the bone and the water is reduced, turn off the heat.
  5. Add Ghee to a seperate pan, and follow the instruction to make Tarka found in steps 3 to 6 of the Saag Recipe
  6. Add the Tarka and mix twice with a large spoon, taking care to not remove the meat from the bone.
  7. Garnish with Coriander and serve with Naan or Roti
  8. Enjoy :)
